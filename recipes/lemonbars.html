Lemon Bars to cure the world

These lemon bars are easy to make and easy to make an impression on your friends and family!

Ingredients

  • 2 Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Buttah
  • luv

Steps

  1. make crust
  2. bake crust 15 mins
  3. While crust is baking make filling
  4. Take crust out, pour filling over and bake for additional 40 mins
